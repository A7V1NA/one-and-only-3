About Us

Us three students of Dev Samaj Modern School, along with help from Richa Mohan Ma'am, have decided that we will do the utmost in our abilities to share the idea of millet-based diets as far as we can. We first decided on this idea with the help of Richa ma'am for the national science congress. Eventually after reaching the state level, we decided to project the same idea in the science exhibition 2024. We will continue to spread awareness and encourage people as we go on. We will be highly grateful if you share our idea with others around you. Thank you!
